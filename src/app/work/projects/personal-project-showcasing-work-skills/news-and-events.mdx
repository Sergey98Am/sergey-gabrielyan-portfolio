## Overview

Implemented pages with dynamic filtering and pagination without a full page reload, with the URL showing filter and pagination parameters. Each item automatically displays related News & Events based on shared categories, and both can also belong to Faculties/Departments/Programs. Events include timezone selection to avoid incorrect times when events happen in different time zones.

## Sitewide Option

The Sitewide option allows a news or event to appear in the general site listings. If a news or event belongs to an academic unit, selecting the Sitewide checkbox makes it visible across the main site. If it is not linked to an academic unit, Sitewide is enabled automatically and the checkbox is hidden.

## Timezone Selection (Events)

Events require selecting the correct timezone during creation. This ensures the event time is stored and displayed accurately, especially for international events. Selecting the proper timezone prevents issues such as incorrect sorting, events appearing at the wrong hour, or mismatched times between the editor’s local timezone and the event’s actual location.

## Drupal Technical Details

News and Events are independent content types (nodes) with their own standalone pages, and they are also enabled as Group relationships for Faculty/Department/Program groups, allowing each academic unit to have its own scoped News and Events.
