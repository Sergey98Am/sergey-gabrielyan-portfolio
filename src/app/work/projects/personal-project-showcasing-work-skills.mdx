## Overview

Development of the personal university project using skills gained from work experience and new learnings.

## Structure

Faculties can have Departments, and both can have Educational Programs (Programs are required to belong to a Faculty, but may optionally belong to a Department). Each one has its own menus, pages, and blocks such as News and Events. There are also global News and Events that are not tied to a specific Faculty/Department/Program.
